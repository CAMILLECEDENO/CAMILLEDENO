Who We Are

KEL DESIGN STUDIO LLC is a boutique, execution-first practice based in Park Ridge, Illinois. We partner with organizations that operate under compliance, safety, or deep technical complexity. Our team blends brand strategy with design ops so approvals are painless and outcomes are measurable. We’ve built medical device UI libraries that pass audits, wayfinding systems that shorten patient travel time, and packaging spec sheets manufacturers actually use. We work directly with engineering, legal, and procurement to remove risk and deliver clarity.

Specialties that move needles

01 — Regulated Brand Systems

From logo to label copy

We build brand toolkits for finance & healthcare where every pixel may be evidence. Deliverables include nomenclature rules, compliant color systems (WCAG AA/AAA), and production-ready templates mapped to your real communication channels.

02 — Product & Data UX

Interfaces for high-stakes decisions

Dashboards and workflows for IoT, lab instrumentation, and B2B SaaS. Expect information hierarchy, alert design, and micro-interactions documented in a living design system engineers can extend.

03 — Packaging & Environmental

From dielines to doors

How we work — a three-step, low-risk path

Discovery Sprint

Two weeks to map stakeholders, constraints, and success metrics. We inventory assets and review compliance requirements to remove unknowns early.

Design & Prove

We prototype key flows/components, test with real users or field staff, and validate accessibility and production methods before scaling.

Spec & Rollout

Finalized systems, tokenized libraries, and vendor-ready packages. We support implementation and post-launch measurement.
